WELCOME TO NAIROBI

the heart of kenya

introduction

nairobi is the capital city of kenya and also one of the first growing cities in east africa

nairobi history

nairobi was founded in 1899 as railway depot on the uganda railway.it quickly expanded and became ;

  • the capital of british
  • the capital of independence for kenya
  • the major hub for trade

nature meets city

nairobi is the only city in the world with national park within its borders

    top natural attractions
  • nairobi national-home to lions,rhinos,giraffes and zebras
  • girrafe center-a sanctuary for endangering rothschild giraffes
  • karura forest-urban forest for walking,cycling and yoga

culture and the people

nairobi is a melting pot of over 40 kenyans tribes and many global cultures ie

  • hindu
  • somalis
